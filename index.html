Expected Delivery Sheet – Actions against delivery discrepancies

  • All discrepancies with deliveries MUST be noted and a reason given. Supply Chain MUST be informed as soon as is reasonably practicable by email AND Face to Face
  • FLM responsible for shift handovers Night – Mornings - MUST take responsibility for communicating delivery issues and enter onto wipe board in service centre
  • FLM can access Red Prairie on parceldeck – pot wash. No delivery should be left unchecked. All MUST be checked for damage, quality, quantity, dates etc.
